Refund Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Try before you buy

Every new account gets a 7-day free trial with full features and no card required — we encourage you to use it to decide whether LeftOff is right for you before paying.

Refunds

  • Monthly plans: if LeftOff doesn't work as described and we can't fix it, contact us within 7 days of the charge for a full refund.
  • Yearly plans: full refund if requested within 14 days of the charge.
  • Lifetime purchases: full refund if requested within 14 days of the charge.
  • Renewal charges are refundable within the same windows if you haven't meaningfully used the Service since the renewal.

How to request

Email dhusiashubham@gmail.com from your registered email with your LeftOff ID and the reason. Refunds are processed to the original payment method within 5–10 business days by our payment providers (Razorpay in India, Dodo Payments internationally).

Cancellation

Cancelling stops future charges; you keep Premium access until the end of the period you already paid for. Cancellation alone does not trigger a refund of the current period unless it falls within the windows above.
